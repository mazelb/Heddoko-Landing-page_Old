About

Heddoko is cutting-edge, smart compression garment that continuously tracks your body movement in order to guide you toward optimal performance and precision in sports.

    It Tracks More than just vital signs

Benefits

01

Live Training Feedback

Real Time Coaching

Heddoko is your training partner and coaching companion. Embedded sensors will monitor your every movement while the Heddoko app analyzes and assesses them in 3D on your smart device.

  • Model your movement in 3D on your smart device.

  • Get Real time instructions and feedback.

  • Get Statistical analysis of your performance.

02

Anywhere Anytime

Be your own coach

You are not limited to your coach's schedule. You are not limited to your Gym's schedule. Just suit up and train as if your coach was there.

  • Coach yourself anytime anywhere

  • Compare your performance to peers
