About

Heddoko is cutting-edge, wearable sports technology that continuously tracks your body movement in order to guide you toward optimal performance and precision in sports. Heddoko is not another glorified pedometer, it is your training partner and coaching companion. Embedded sensors will monitor your every movement while the Heddoko app analyzes and assesses them in 3D on your smart device. As a result, Heddoko provides meaningful feedback that will alleviate incorrect movements, thus reducing the risk of injury all while increasing your understanding of your own body.
