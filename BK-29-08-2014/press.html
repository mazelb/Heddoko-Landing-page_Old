Heddoko

Heddoko is the first smart sports garment that sees your movement in 3D and provides live visual feedback to both your web dashboard and your mobile device to optimize precision performance and prevent injury.

It is not another glorified pedometer; it is a training partner and coaching companion. Embedded sensors monitor the movement while the Heddoko app analyzes and assesses the data and provides live 3D feedback directly to the users mobile dashboard.

Through the Heddoko app incorrect movements are immediately detected and suggestive 3D feedback is provided to alleviate the chance of injury and optimize performance. Heddoko is more than just a coach, it's your own personal performance and injury prevention center. It's designed for those who want to excel in sports, master their body and not be limited by the scheduling and financial commitment of a personal trainer.

Motion capture technology was always limited to the gaming and entertainment industries and Heddoko is breaking that barrier for consumers. Heddoko was founded in May 2014 in Montreal, Quebec. The founder, Mazen Elbawab is an experienced developer from the gaming industry and motion capture.
