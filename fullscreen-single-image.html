About

Heddoko is cutting-edge, wearable sports technology that continuously tracks your body movement in order to guide you toward optimal performance and precision in sports. Heddoko is not another glorified pedometer, it is your training partner and coaching companion. Embedded sensors will monitor your every movement while the Heddoko app analyzes and assesses them in 3D on your smart device. As a result, Heddoko provides meaningful feedback that will alleviate incorrect movements reducing the risk of injury all while increasing your understanding of your own body.

Features

Unlike the general wearable trend of counting steps, heart beats and calories, Heddoko's technology enables real-time tracking of the body delivering accurate measurment of joint angles, positions and velocity.
