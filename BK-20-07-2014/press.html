Heddoko

Heddoko is a sports garment that continuously tracks the athleteâ€™s body movement to guide him/her toward optimal performance and precision in sports. It is not another glorified pedometer; it is a training partner and coaching companion. Embedded sensors will monitor the movement while the Heddoko app analyzes and assesses them in 3D on their smart devices. As a result they will get meaningful feedback that will alleviate incorrect movements reducing the risk of injury all while increasing their understanding of their own body. The garment is designed to move fluidly with the athlete and comfortably help him with his performance. Motion capture technology was always limited to the gaming and entertainment industries and Heddoko is breaking that barrier for consumers. Heddoko was founded in May 2014 in Montreal, Quebec. The founder, Mazen Elbawab is an experienced developer from the gaming industry and motion capture.
